# How to Run Your Mobile App

Congratulations! Your basic mobile app has been set up using React Native and Expo.

## Prerequisites

1.  **Node.js**: Make sure you have Node.js installed (you already have it!).
2.  **Expo Go App**: Download the "Expo Go" app on your iOS or Android device from the App Store or Google Play Store.

## Running the App

1.  Open your terminal and navigate to the project directory:
    ```bash
    cd my-mobile-app
    ```
2.  Start the development server:
    ```bash
    npm start
    ```
3.  **To view on your phone:**
    *   Open the terminal where you ran `npm start`. You will see a large QR code.
    *   **Android:** Open the Expo Go app and tap "Scan QR code".
    *   **iOS:** Open the built-in Camera app on your iPhone and scan the QR code. Tap the notification to open it in Expo Go.
    *   *Note: Ensure your phone and PC are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.*

4.  **To view on the web:**
    *   Press the `w` key in your terminal after running `npm start`.
